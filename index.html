About

We are a team formed by PhDs in mathematical optimization and machine learning. By means of the most modern mathematical and algorithmic techniques, we provide high-quality services in these areas.

Mathematical Optimization

In brief, mathematical optimization comes into play when big and complex decision problems have to be addressed. E.g., those involving uncertainty or a large number of decision variables and/or constraints to be satisfied. With mathematical optimization, you will not only solve any decision problem, but will end up with the optimal strategy to apply.

Machine learning

Machine learning is the art of making computers to learn from experience so they get better over time at a given task, such as prediction or classification. Using state-of-the-art algorithms, you will succeed in complex tasks such as product recommendation or risk assignment.

Services

Our services go from providing solutions in our areas of expertise, to help companies to incorporate optimization and machine learning tools into their businesses. We provide them with the mathematical models, algorithms and codes they need to succeed in this task.
